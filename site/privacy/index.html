Privacy

Privacy should be clear from the start

This policy explains what SchedAI uses, what stays on your device, and what only happens when you choose to turn it on.

Who this policy covers

This policy covers the SchedAI app and the SchedAI website. If we expand the product later, we will update this page so it stays current.

Who runs SchedAI

SchedAI is operated by Adeoluwa Adekoya.

What SchedAI stores on your device

SchedAI stores your tasks, planning preferences, reminder settings, work window, calendar sync choice, and your display name on your device. This information is used so the app can work the way you expect from one session to the next.

Calendar access

If you choose to enable Calendar sync, SchedAI asks for Calendar permission so it can create and update events in a dedicated SchedAI calendar. SchedAI may also read event times from readable calendars to avoid scheduling tasks over existing commitments. It uses those times as busy blocks and does not store event titles from your other calendars. If you do not turn Calendar sync on, SchedAI does not read from or write to your Calendar.

Microphone and speech recognition

If you use voice planning, SchedAI asks for Microphone and Speech Recognition permission so spoken input can be turned into text for task planning. If you do not use voice planning, SchedAI does not need those permissions.

Optional AI parsing

SchedAI can preview tasks on device first. If you choose to improve a preview with AI, the task text you typed or spoke, the planning date, your locale, time zone, and an offline preview are sent to SchedAI's hosted parsing service and processed by OpenAI to return structured tasks. SchedAI also sends a rotating app-side client identifier so the service can prevent abuse, apply rate limits, and disable hosted AI for a specific app install if needed. This is used only to provide the task parsing feature. Do not include sensitive information in task text that you do not want processed by the hosted AI service.

Hosted AI improve is optional. SchedAI asks before first use, and you can turn it back off later in the app's Settings. SchedAI is designed not to store your submitted task text in a separate app database after parsing, but limited technical records or provider-side processing records may still exist for security, abuse prevention, and reliability.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, SchedAI asks for notification permission so it can send local reminders before tasks start. If reminders are off, no local reminders are scheduled.

Sign in with Apple

If you choose to share your Apple-provided name, SchedAI stores that display name locally on your device so the app can greet you by name. At launch, this flow is used only for local personalization inside the app. It does not create a SchedAI cloud account.

What SchedAI does not do at launch

At launch, SchedAI is designed around local planning with optional hosted AI parsing when you choose to use it. We do not use ads inside the app. We do not sell your personal information. If we add new cloud features later, this policy will be updated before those changes go live.

Website data

The website may receive basic technical information needed to load pages and protect the service. If analytics, forms, or mailing list tools are added later, this page will be updated to reflect that.

Your choices

  • You can keep Calendar sync off.
  • You can keep reminders off.
  • You can choose not to use voice planning.
  • You can decline or later turn off hosted AI improve in Settings.
  • You can remove app data from your device by deleting the app.

Retention and deletion

Most SchedAI planning data is stored locally on your device unless you choose features that require system or hosted services. If you want to stop future hosted AI processing, you can turn hosted AI improve off in Settings. If you have questions about deletion or privacy choices, contact schedaisupport@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact schedaisupport@gmail.com.
