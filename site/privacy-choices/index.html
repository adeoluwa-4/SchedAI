Privacy Choices

Control what leaves your device

SchedAI is designed to work locally first. These choices explain how to keep optional services off or review them again later.

Hosted AI improve

Hosted AI improve is optional. Before first use, SchedAI explains what task data may be sent to SchedAI's hosted parser and OpenAI. You can turn this back off later in the app's Settings under Privacy & Support.

Calendar access

Calendar sync can stay off. If you enable it, SchedAI may read busy times from readable calendars and write planned tasks into a dedicated SchedAI calendar.

Voice planning

Voice planning needs both Speech Recognition and Microphone permission. If you never start voice planning, those permissions are not needed.

Reminders

Reminders are optional. If they are off, SchedAI does not schedule local task alerts.

Questions or deletion requests

Contact schedaisupport@gmail.com if you have privacy questions or want help understanding your choices.
