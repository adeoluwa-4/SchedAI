A few things people usually ask

The short version is simple. SchedAI is meant to feel lightweight, respectful, and easy to trust.

Do I have to connect my Calendar

No. Calendar sync is optional and only happens if you turn it on inside the app.

Do I have to use voice

No. Voice planning is there when it helps. You can still work with tasks manually.

What happens when I sign in

Sign in with Apple lets the app greet you by name so the experience feels a bit more personal.
