Quick answers

Calendar sync is optional and can be turned on from Settings. Reminders are optional too. Voice planning needs Microphone and Speech Recognition permission before it can work. Hosted AI improve is optional and can be turned off later in Settings.

Privacy choices

You can review hosted AI, calendar, reminder, and voice choices in the app's Privacy & Support settings or on the privacy choices page.

Launch list questions

If you want to update or remove your launch list details, email schedaisupport@gmail.com and we can help.

If Calendar sync is not working

Open Settings in the app and confirm Calendar sync is enabled. If needed, check iPhone Settings and confirm SchedAI has Calendar access.

If reminders are not showing up

Confirm reminders are enabled in the app and that SchedAI has notification permission on your device. You may also want to check that your tasks have scheduled start times.

If voice planning is not available

SchedAI needs both Speech Recognition and Microphone access for voice planning. If either permission is denied, the voice flow will not start.
